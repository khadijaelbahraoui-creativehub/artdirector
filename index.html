Int. About — Introduction
K

A cinematic eye,
a designer's discipline.

I'm a Senior Art Director & Graphic Designer with 8+ years of experience spanning creative agencies in Milan — LFM Agency and De Santis — an international film production with Banijay France for Netflix, and freelance work across Switzerland.

I hold a Bachelor in Graphic Design & Art Direction and a Master in Cinematography & Storytelling from Accademia di Belle Arti Milano. That dual training shows in how I work: every brand identity is built like a story, with a beginning, a visual rhythm, and a reason to keep watching.

Two of my film credits are catalogued on IMDb: Der Schlüssel (2020, dir. Ghassan Jaradat) and Rest in Piece (2022, dir. Antoine Antabi).
